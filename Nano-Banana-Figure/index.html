Nano-Banana Figure-Edit LoRA (Real/Anime/Pet → Figure) for Qwen-Image-Edit

Nano-Banana-Figure is compact LoRA adapter that specializes Qwen-Image-Edit for a specific editing task: converting real people, anime characters, and pets into figure/collectible (PVC/resin “garage kit”) style images. We curated 100 high-quality paired examples using our Nano-Banana pipeline (powered by Gemini 2.5 Flash – Image Generation), then fine-tuned Qwen-Image-Edit to better preserve identity while applying realistic figure materials, lighting, and product-shot composition.
